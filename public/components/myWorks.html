The Hiring Hub

The Hiring Hub is a transformative recruitment platform designed to revolutionize the talent acquisition process at XYZ Corporation. Built with technologies like Node.js and MongoDB, this platform integrates job postings, candidate sourcing, and onboarding into a unified system. By incorporating AI-driven shortlisting and automated processes, it reduces the manual workload of HR teams and enhances the candidate experience. The Hiring Hub also includes tools for tracking performance metrics and planning succession, helping align talent management with the company's strategic goals. This project is set to modernize recruitment, ensuring efficiency and competitiveness in the job market.
